Offline Mode

OralSync needs a connection to load live clinic data.

The app shell is installed, but patient records, appointments, billing, uploads, and other live modules still come from the cloud.

Reconnect to the internet, then reopen OralSync to continue working with the latest data.

Clinic-safe caching: OralSync only caches the app shell and static assets.

Live patient and business data are not stored offline by this PWA setup.
